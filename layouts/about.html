Our Story

Founded in 2020, Moon Light Cafe began as a dream to create a space where people could escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and find comfort in the simple pleasure of a perfectly crafted cup of coffee.

Today, we continue to honor that vision by serving not just coffee, but moments of joy, connection, and inspiration. Every visit to Moon Light Cafe is an opportunity to create memories that last a lifetime.
